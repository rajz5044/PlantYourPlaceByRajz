How We Started?

We were on a educational tour to Thailand. The best part of the trip was we were free to explore everything in Thailand. We explored many thing. We discovered the business of BONSAI PLANTS in Thailand was very successfull. We planned the same thing to open a small start-up of a bonsai plant company. The decision was made everything was ready but we were not able to find any place to open our small store. So we decided to built a website to sell the plants. Every one us are in IT field so it was very easy for us to work with the store on the base of internet. Our store was totally online during the initial phases. Gradually after the peak progess we invest the profit to built the physical store for our brand.
