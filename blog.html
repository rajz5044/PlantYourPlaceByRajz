Technology

As we all know that technology is the most useful thing in our daily life.Today's world is fulled with technology. We depends upon technology. In today's world technology is advancing day by day which has made our life easier. Technology,in general refers to all the tools , devices and the machines which we use in our daily life. It is also known as the application of scientific knowledge. Every devices that we are surrounded by is example of technology. Technology has made our lives way more easier most of our work is done through various technology. As the world is being developed the technolgy has take the place in a rapid way. It has made our life way more earier that we depends more in technology rather than a human beings.

Impact of technology in our daily life

Technology affects the way people communicate, learn and think. Techn ology has affected human beings in many things like in economy, children,developments and many more. Another way technology has impacted society is through communication, how we talk and communicate with one another worldwide. Technology brought many new methods of electronic communication. For example, there are emails, social networking and so on. The impact of technology is all around us. We depends on technolgy to improve our living standard. We live in an age where technological advancement are common. Almost five year ago mobile phone were primarily used for calling and texting but today the uses of mobile phones are very varied. The advent of smartphones has greatly simplified our lives.Smartphones and apps have changed the way we work, communicate and play. Technological innovations have a huge impact on society, and there is debate as to where these changes are beneficial.

Technology has also led to an improvement in access to educational resources. The internet has made it possible for students to do their work anytime,anywhere. In educational field,the impact of technology is largely positive. It has made learning more interactive and collaborative, and it has improved access to resources. Technology is making the education improve over time with technology, students and parents have a variety of learning tools at their fingerprints. Technology has also impacted education by serving as an essential teching aid and facilitating the student's aaimilation of knowledge.Education has been made easier, accessible, and comprehensive than the traditional learning system, which had little technological advancements. Despite the fact thst we cannot really ignore that there are a number of ways in which technology negatively impacts our society, for the better part it has greately helped to make our lives better.
